## 适用目标

Use as primary only when the artifact's main job is to communicate a brand, idea,
product launch, event/project story, or editorial subject to an external or broad
audience and create credible, memorable, and controlled understanding or action.

Select this Profile from the real external communication objective, central message,
story angle, identity, claims, and publication/use boundary. A desire for a beautiful
page, brand colors, a Logo, an “annual report” label, or `presentation` use mode does
not by itself establish this Profile.

## 排除范围

Use `live-presentation-persuasion` when presenter rhythm and real-time spoken audience
movement are the dominant product. Use `formal-submission-writing` or
`rule-response-application-defense` when formal receipt, authoritative eligibility,
mandatory response, scoring, or submission constraints decide success. Use
`teaching-training-knowledge-transfer` when learner capability and knowledge transfer
are dominant.

Do not implement a public website, hosting, CMS, social publishing, email or ad
delivery, campaign orchestration, tracking, user analytics, conversion measurement,
content calendar, brand-management system, or publication workflow. Do not create a
new theme merely because this Profile was selected.

## 成品

A customer-usable high-quality offline editorial HTML artifact or presentation-ready
brand deck within the current delivery boundary, with:

- correct brand/entity/product/project identity and a validated visual binding;
- an audience-relevant central message and story whose claim strength remains honest;
- approved or source-backed claims, controlled editorial framing, and visible limits;
- source and usage-rights provenance for real, public, generated, and illustrative
  assets;
- a verified CTA/action path only when the confirmed goal requires one; and
- the existing current-file authorization, objective QA, Handoff, delivery, and
  `《待核实内容清单》` boundaries.

The result has no required campaign structure, chapter count, page count, magazine
layout, poster treatment, card system, CTA page, or content formula. TaoHtml delivery
does not mean the artifact has been hosted, published, distributed, promoted, tracked,
or converted an audience.

## 所需信息

Reuse eligible conversation, bound sources, the confirmed Material Understanding
Summary, and still-supported Handoff state before identifying a gap. Maintain the
shared `known | confirmed | inferred | missing` ledger; do not expose the following
as a brand questionnaire, copyright checklist, publication form, or fixed campaign
brief.

### 受众、传播目标与身份状态

Resolve only what is needed to shape the communication honestly:

- external or broad audience, their current context, and the understanding, memory,
  trust, or action the artifact should create;
- communication/publication setting or channel only as a design and distribution
  constraint, never as a claim that TaoHtml will publish there;
- central message, story angle, relevant tension, and what the audience should retain;
- brand, entity, product, project, or event identity; approved naming and relationship
  among identities; and
- tone, visual binding, distribution restriction, and responsibility/approval boundary.

Real brand names, Logos, brand bars, headers/footers, product identity, and project
identity must come from confirmed visual or customer material. Reference
reconstruction and corporate-template fidelity continue through the existing visual
workflow; this Profile does not create, infer, or silently switch a theme.

### 主张与出版状态

Classify material communication statements at their real strength:

- `approved claim`;
- `source-backed fact`;
- `interpretation / editorial framing`;
- `projection`;
- `illustrative content`; and
- `unverified claim`.

Do not invent an award, ranking, customer, case, quotation, testimonial, sales result,
market position, performance effect, partner, contact detail, product availability,
launch state, or publication state. Editorial confidence and visual impact cannot
promote an interpretation, projection, illustration, or unverified claim.

### 素材、权利与真实资产保护

For every material asset, record source and usage boundary at the level required by
the intended use:

- customer-provided, public/third-party, licensed or otherwise usable, generated, or
  illustrative source status;
- exact locator or bound local identity, inspection coverage, author/owner where
  known, and current usage/permission basis;
- restrictions on modification, attribution, territory, medium, duration, or
  distribution where actually established; and
- whether a real Logo, screenshot, photograph, product image, or other identity-
  bearing asset must remain unaltered.

Access to an image does not equal permission to use it. Do not replace or materially
alter a real Logo, screenshot, photograph, or evidence-bearing visual with generated
content, and do not use generation to change a factual event or identity.

### 引用、证言与行动路径

Bind a quotation or testimonial to its exact original text, named or authorized
identity, permission/public status, context, and locator. A paraphrase, translation,
anonymous composite, or editorial synthesis must not be presented as a verbatim real
quotation.

Add a CTA or conversion path only when the confirmed communication objective requires
one. Reuse the shared desired-action/real-action-path contract. A link, QR code,
email, phone number, download, registration, purchase, booking, or contact path must
be exact, usable in the actual delivery environment, and verified without performing
the external action. Do not force a sales CTA onto an awareness, identity, or neutral
editorial result.

### publication-ready 与降级边界

Call the artifact `public / publication-ready` only when the identity, material key
claims, asset usage boundaries, promised distribution conditions, and every required
action path are verified for actual use. A disclosure or `《待核实内容清单》` records
residual risk; it cannot upgrade an unresolved identity, claim, right, permission, or
action path into publication readiness.

When any critical boundary is unresolved, block a public/publication-ready conclusion
or continue only after the user explicitly changes the result to `internal review /
editorial draft`. That draft must remain marked not for publication or external use in
the brief, every outward-facing status or closing page, delivery wording, and Handoff.

### 最大缺口规则

After each pass, ask at most the one largest still-missing item whose answer could
change audience understanding, claim legality or credibility, asset usability,
identity, publication readiness, or the promised action result. Reuse the shared 0-6
question budget, repetition rule, information-gain stop, and hard boundaries; do not
add a brand questionnaire, rights checklist, or independent confirmation round.

Route low-risk editorial transitions, non-factual framing, and clearly illustrative
expression to output-first production. Never creatively complete a real identity,
right, permission, quotation, testimonial, customer, result, key claim, contact,
action path, product availability, launch state, or publication-ready conclusion.

## design-ready 条件

The shared design-ready gate must pass, plus these communication decisions must be
sufficiently clear for the honest delivery class:

- external/broad audience, communication or publication setting, central objective,
  central message, story angle, memory structure, and responsibility boundary;
- brand/entity/product/project identity, naming, tone, validated visual binding, and
  restrictions on external use or distribution;
- every key approved/source-backed/unverified claim, editorial interpretation,
  projection, illustrative item, contradiction, and current support strength;
- material asset identity, source/provenance, inspection coverage, usage-rights or
  permission boundary, attribution/restriction, and real-asset protection need;
- exact quotation/testimonial text, identity, permission/public status, context, and
  locator where used;
- desired action and exact verified real action path only when a CTA or conversion
  objective actually exists; and
- public/publication-ready or internal/editorial-draft status, critical gaps, risks,
  distribution limits, and what the artifact does not establish.

Publication readiness is design-ready only when identity, key claims, rights, real
assets, and promised action path reach actual usable status. An `internal review /
editorial draft` may become design-ready when its checked scope and gaps are clear,
but it must not be packaged as public or publication-ready.

### 设计简报增量

Write these customer-readable fields inside the one existing Report Design Brief's
adaptive `场景特有决策` section. Reuse the current ledger; do not create a second
brief, brand manual, publication plan, rights checklist, authorization, or
Profile-specific confirmation round.

- `外部受众与传播/出版场景`: audience, context/channel as a design constraint,
  intended understanding or action, and actual distribution boundary;
- `核心传播目标、central message 与 story angle`: central objective, message,
  tension, story angle, and intended memory structure;
- `品牌/主体身份、语气与视觉边界`: exact identity/naming, relationship among
  entities/products/projects, tone, validated visual binding, and protected elements;
- `approved/source-backed/unverified claims`: key claims, current classification,
  source/approval status, interpretation, projection, illustration, and limits;
- `素材来源、权利与真实资产保护`: asset identity, provenance, usage or permission
  basis, attribution/restriction, modification limit, and real-asset protection;
- `引用/证言状态`: exact text, identity, context, permission/public state, locator,
  and whether any wording is a paraphrase or synthesis;
- `CTA/action path`: only when applicable, with desired action, exact path, source,
  verification result, rendered form, and offline/environment limitation;
- `公开/内部使用边界、分发限制`: public/publication-ready or `internal review /
  editorial draft`, permitted use, prohibited use, channel/distribution limits, and
  approval responsibility; and
- `关键缺口、风险与交付边界`: unresolved identity/claim/right/permission/path,
  external-use risk, draft label, unresolved items, and what TaoHtml does not publish
  or measure.

Record reversible inferences and their bases in the existing `待确认项`. Display and
confirm the complete current brief once. Profile selection, complete-brief
confirmation, and current-file Production Authorization remain three independent
facts.

## 叙事任务

### Golden Path

1. **Enter through shared state.** Establish or inherit the material route, task
   intent, use mode, content length, source bindings, and exactly one primary Profile.
   Do not route here from beauty, brand colors, a Logo, annual-report naming, or
   presentation mode alone, and do not reopen known horizontal values.
2. **Bind audience, identity, and communication outcome.** Establish the audience,
   setting, central objective, central message, story angle, identity, tone, visual
   binding, and external-use boundary. Ask only the current largest result-changing
   gap when one remains.
3. **Audit claims before amplification.** Separate approved claims, source-backed
   facts, editorial framing, projections, illustrations, and unverified claims.
   Preserve contradictions and limits; never use tone or visual impact to raise claim
   strength.
4. **Audit assets and rights.** Bind each real/public/generated/illustrative asset to
   provenance, inspection coverage, usage or permission basis, attribution and
   modification limits. Protect real Logos, screenshots, photographs, product images,
   and evidence-bearing visuals from substitution or factual alteration.
5. **Verify quotations and optional action.** Preserve the exact identity, text,
   permission/public state, context, and locator of quotations/testimonials. Add an
   action only when the goal requires it, and use the exact verified path without
   forcing a sales CTA.
6. **Choose the honest publication branch.** Use public/publication-ready only when
   identity, key claims, asset rights, distribution boundary, and required action path
   are usable. Otherwise block or use only the explicitly accepted `internal review /
   editorial draft`, preserving the not-for-publication boundary through pages,
   delivery, and Handoff.
7. **Build the communication sequence from the real goal.** Usually perform the jobs
   of earning attention → establishing relevance → expressing the central message →
   building credibility through proof, story, and identity → creating a memorable
   structure → confirming meaning or a verified action. Use only the jobs this result
   needs; do not turn them into a fixed marketing or editorial template.
8. **Assign page roles without theme substitution.** A page may serve hook, context,
   message, proof, identity, story, editorial transition, action, or limitation. Use
   the confirmed visual system; selecting this Profile does not automatically create
   a magazine style, collage, poster, card system, or CTA page.
9. **Use the one shared brief and authorization chain.** Add only the fields above to
   the current Report Design Brief, obtain its ordinary complete-brief confirmation,
   then pass current-file Production Authorization. Do not add another confirmation
   or treat Profile selection as production authority.
10. **Produce through the existing chain.** After authorization, follow
    `process-playbook.md`, save the first runnable direct-HTML artifact, apply the
    validated visual route and current Runtime, then run specialized plus shared QA
    and delivery gates.

### 页面任务与传播表达

Give each page one real communication job, such as hook, context, central message,
proof, identity, story, editorial transition, action, or limitation. Use the
composition that serves that job and the confirmed visual system. These are semantic
tasks, not fixed chapters, posters, collage pages, cards, CTA pages, or layouts.

Editorial illustration, generated imagery, simulation, and projection must remain
distinct from real facts, photographs, evidence, customers, and cases. Visual impact
may strengthen attention and memory, but it cannot strengthen the underlying claim.

## 证据规则

- Bind every material brand/entity/product/project identity, key claim, customer,
  case, data point, award, ranking, market-position statement, performance effect,
  partner, product availability, launch state, and publication state to eligible
  evidence or explicit approval at its actual strength.
- Keep approved claim, source-backed fact, editorial interpretation, projection,
  illustrative content, and unverified claim distinct across titles, visuals, body,
  sources, summaries, and closing language.
- Record asset provenance separately from usage rights. Accessibility, download, or
  public visibility does not equal permission to use, modify, or distribute an asset.
- Never replace, redraw, or factually alter a real Logo, screenshot, photograph,
  product image, event record, or evidence-bearing visual with generated content.
  Generated or illustrative assets must carry the shared labels and delivery record.
- Bind quotations and testimonials to exact text, identity, permission/public state,
  context, and locator. Never package a paraphrase, translation, anonymous composite,
  or editorial synthesis as a real verbatim quotation.
- For a CTA, preserve the exact verified link, decoded QR value, email, phone number,
  command, contact, booking, download, registration, or purchase path. Never invent
  or “improve” it, and never imply that TaoHtml executed the external action.
- Apply the shared source-role/availability, high-risk verification, output-first,
  and `《待核实内容清单》` rules without using disclosure to cross identity, claim,
  rights, action-path, or publication-readiness boundaries.

## 横向参数默认值

- `input_entry_route`: inherit the established material route
- `use_mode`: `reading` recommendation only after explicit delegation; otherwise inherit or resolve the existing startup choice
- `visual_binding`: inherit the validated visual route
- `evidence_rigor`: `standard`
- `information_density`: `medium`
- `motion_density`: `moderate`
- `continuation_state`: inherit the Handoff decision

These defaults never override a known, confirmed, inherited, or explicitly delegated
horizontal value. `content_length` remains the shared independent choice. An external
audience or brand deck label never silently selects a visual system or use mode.

## IR 映射边界

Direct HTML remains the default. Only when the IR engineering route is independently
authorized may this Profile guide existing editorial narrative, claim, evidence,
source, image/asset, citation, page-task, action-path, Projection, and unresolved-item
semantics.

Do not add brand, campaign, publication, claim-approval, rights, quotation-permission,
CTA, conversion, analytics, hosting, or distribution fields to the Report IR Schema.
Do not create a fixed JSON campaign model, CMS, publication engine, tracker,
Profile-triggered IR path, Compiler branch, or Validator bypass. The customer-readable
communication and publication state belongs in the confirmed Report Design Brief and
project-local working record; any authorized IR remains downstream.

## Runtime/主题使用

Use only the established reading or single-screen presentation mode, validated
built-in/project/enterprise visual route, current page semantics, and current Runtime.
Existing static composition and `fragment-v1` may support attention and spoken order
when the confirmed mode requires them.

The Profile does not add a brand or editorial theme, CMS, public website, hosting,
social/email/ad publishing, tracking, analytics, conversion measurement, dual-screen
presenter view, cross-page morphing, complex state, or new Runtime control. Selecting
the Profile never switches the confirmed visual binding to an editorial collage.

## QA 验收

Run all existing objective QA, traceability, asset/browser QA, current-file
authorization rechecks, Runtime/editor checks where applicable, Handoff validation,
and delivery gates. In addition, verify:

- brand/entity/product/project identity, naming, Logo, brand bar, header/footer, tone,
  and validated visual binding remain consistent and real assets are not altered or
  replaced;
- every key claim, quotation, testimonial, customer, case, data point, award, ranking,
  market position, effect, partner, availability, launch, and publication statement
  matches its source/approval strength and exact locator;
- each asset records provenance, inspection coverage, usage/permission status,
  attribution/restriction, and generated/illustrative labels; accessibility has not
  been treated as usage permission;
- quotation/testimonial text, identity, permission/public state, context, locator, and
  paraphrase/translation boundary remain exact;
- every applicable CTA/action path has matching visible text, link or decoded QR,
  intended destination, source, environment, and real accessibility, while a report
  without an action objective has no forced sales CTA;
- `internal review / editorial draft` remains not for publication or external use
  throughout status pages, closing language, delivery wording, and Handoff, and
  disclosure has not upgraded it to public/publication-ready;
- offline assets, browser behavior, current-file authorization, Handoff, and delivery
  completeness pass on the exact artifact without claiming hosting, publication,
  distribution, tracking, analytics, or conversion; and
- editorial or generated visuals never replace factual evidence or increase claim
  strength.

Any failure remains a Profile-specific QA failure in addition to the shared gate; it
does not replace or collapse existing QA and delivery conclusions.

## 能力叠加与冲突处理

Teaching explanation, research support, project detail, formal writing, or live
delivery may be bounded overlays with a stated source Profile, reason, and affected
scope. They may not import another Profile's complete intake, design-ready gate,
narrative sequence, artifact set, or QA workflow.

Keep this Profile primary while external/broad-audience brand or editorial
communication remains the dominant product. Switch to
`live-presentation-persuasion` when live stage rhythm dominates,
`formal-submission-writing` or `rule-response-application-defense` when formal or
authoritative rules decide success, or `teaching-training-knowledge-transfer` when
learner capability dominates. Rebuild only affected decisions, reuse the existing
complete-brief confirmation, and never run two Golden Paths in full.
