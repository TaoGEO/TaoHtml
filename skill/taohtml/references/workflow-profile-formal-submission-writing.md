## 适用目标

Use as primary when a defined recipient or reporting body will formally receive,
use, circulate, or archive the artifact for an institutional purpose, and success
depends on complete required content, controlled terminology and factual wording,
cross-section consistency, and an explicit responsibility/sign-off boundary.

The Profile is selected by the real recipient, institutional purpose, formal-use
boundary, and acceptance expectations. A formal tone, official-looking visual
language, memo filename, or `reading` use mode is not sufficient by itself.

## 排除范围

Do not use as primary when authoritative eligibility, application, tender, award,
evaluation, scoring, or item-by-item response rules decide success. In that case use
`rule-response-application-defense`, even when the final artifact is also formally
written and archived.

Do not select this Profile for ordinary business writing merely because the customer
wants restrained language. Research reasoning, recurring performance control,
decision selection, teaching, or live persuasion may be the actual primary outcome.
Do not create a fixed institutional chapter template, mandatory page count, formal
document schema, signature workflow, or records-management system.

## 成品

A customer-usable offline formal HTML artifact that:

- addresses the correct formal recipient and institutional purpose;
- covers every actual mandatory chapter, field, statement, and attachment reference;
- preserves authoritative wording, customer facts, external evidence, and Agent
  supplements as distinct source roles and strengths;
- keeps terms, figures, dates, identities, versions, scope, responsibilities,
  conclusions, and attachment references consistent across the artifact; and
- passes the existing current-file authorization, objective QA, delivery evidence,
  Handoff, and `《待核实内容清单》` boundaries.

The result has no required chapter count, page count, card layout, cover treatment,
or fixed sequence independent of the real requirement set.

## 所需信息

Reuse eligible conversation, bound sources, the confirmed Material Understanding
Summary, and still-supported Handoff state before identifying a gap. Maintain the
shared `known | confirmed | inferred | missing` ledger; do not expose the following
as a Profile questionnaire.

### 正式任务状态

Resolve only the information needed to judge the formal artifact honestly:

- exact recipient/reporting body and any distinct readers or distribution groups;
- institutional purpose, requested decision or use, formal-use boundary, and archive
  or record status;
- mandatory chapters, fields, statements, declarations, attachment references, or
  other content explicitly required by a governing source;
- an agreed or customary structure that guides reading but is not itself mandatory;
- controlled terminology, definitions, units, accounting or statistical basis, and
  other declared interpretive conventions;
- authorship, responsibility, approval, sign-off, signature, attribution, seal, and
  closing-name boundaries without assuming authority the customer did not provide;
- applicable date, period, document version, edition, effective basis, and attachment
  version;
- length, format, medium, file, naming, deadline, and submission constraints; and
- confidentiality, classification, retention, and distribution restrictions only
  when a source or the user actually imposes them.

### 要求与来源强度状态

Track the actual requirements and their origins without promoting weak material:

- `权威要求`: an applicable institutional rule, instruction, template, notice, or
  other governing source, with identity, version, scope, and inspection coverage;
- `客户材料`: first-party facts, records, positions, and approved language supplied
  for the current task;
- `外部证据`: independently retrieved public or third-party evidence with the shared
  provenance and verification fields; and
- `Agent 补全`: proposed structure, transitions, explanation, or ordinary creative
  supplements that never acquire official authority through polished expression.

Record whether a content item is mandatory, agreed/customary, optional, or currently
unclear. An ordinary reference, prior example, house style, or current artifact is
not a formal requirement merely because it appears official.

### 最大缺口规则

After each pass, ask at most the one largest still-missing item whose answer could
change mandatory coverage, official meaning, formal recipient/use, conclusion,
responsibility/sign-off, or deliverability. Reuse the shared 0-6 question budget,
repetition rule, information-gain stop, and hard boundaries; do not add a formal-
writing questionnaire or independent confirmation round.

Route ordinary missing transitions, examples, or expression to creative supplements.
Never route a missing mandatory fact, authority, identity, title, date, institutional
rule, signature, commitment, achieved result, or responsibility into creative
completion.

## design-ready 条件

The shared design-ready gate must pass, plus the following result-changing formal
decisions must be sufficiently clear:

- recipient/reporting body, institutional purpose, formal use, archive/distribution
  boundary, and requested conclusion or action;
- every known mandatory content item and its real coverage status;
- each authoritative source's identity, applicability, version/date, and checked
  scope, with ordinary references kept at their actual strength;
- controlled terminology, definitions, units, date/period basis, and other material
  interpretive conventions;
- authorship, responsibility, approval, sign-off, signature, closing-name, and
  attachment responsibility boundaries;
- applicable format, length, file, deadline, and source-backed confidentiality or
  distribution constraints; and
- critical gaps, conflicts, unavailable proof, and risks that affect formal use or
  prevent an accurate deliverable claim.

Mandatory content may not remain silently absent. A mandatory official fact or
authority gap that cannot be truthfully bounded remains a hard gap rather than an
invitation to draft plausible text.

Record reversible inferences and their bases in the existing `待确认项`. Display and

## 叙事任务

### Golden Path

1. **Enter through shared state.** Establish or inherit the material route, task
   intent, use mode, content length, source bindings, and exactly one primary Profile
   under the shared contracts. Do not reopen known choices or route here from formal
   tone alone.
2. **Bind the formal task.** Identify the recipient/reporting body, institutional
   purpose, formal-use and archive/distribution boundary, requested outcome, and
   responsibility/sign-off limits. Ask only the current largest result-changing gap
   when one remains.
3. **Derive real requirement coverage.** Separate mandatory content, agreed/customary
   structure, terminology/conventions, responsibility/sign-off, date/version,
   format/length, deadline, and source-backed confidentiality/distribution rules.
   Trace every actual mandatory item to a planned or visible content location; do not
   replace missing coverage with a polished summary.
4. **Preserve source roles and strength.** Keep authoritative requirements, customer
   materials, external evidence, and Agent completion distinct. Verify the identity,
   version, applicability, and checked scope of authoritative sources. Never promote
   an ordinary reference, example, prior artifact, or uninspected file into an
   official requirement.
5. **Build the formal reading sequence from the task.** Arrange the necessary jobs in
   a reader-usable order such as task/mandate, basis, facts, analysis, conclusion or
   request, responsibility, and follow-up conditions. Use only the jobs the actual
   requirements need; do not force these labels into a fixed chapter template.
6. **Control meaning across sections.** Reconcile terminology, figures, dates,
   identities, versions, scope, responsibility, conclusions, and attachment/citation
   references. Keep source facts, interpretation, and proposed or recommended
   language distinguishable, including in summaries and closing statements.
7. **Use the one shared brief and authorization chain.** Add only the fields above to
   the current Report Design Brief, obtain its ordinary complete-brief confirmation,
   then pass current-file Production Authorization. Do not add another confirmation
   or treat Profile selection as production authority.
8. **Produce through the existing chain.** After authorization, follow
   `process-playbook.md`, save the first runnable direct-HTML artifact, apply the
   validated visual route and current Runtime, then run specialized plus shared QA
   and delivery gates.

### 页面任务与正式阅读重点

Derive page and section roles from the actual requirement set. Useful semantic jobs
may include mandate, requirement context, facts, analysis, conclusion, request,
responsibility, follow-up condition, attachment locator, and formal closure. Make the
recipient, operative dates/versions, responsibility, required statements, and
attachment links easy to locate. These are reading tasks, not required chapters,
tables, cards, or page layouts.

## 证据规则

- Preserve the role and strength of every source. Authority comes from the applicable
  governing source, not from formal typography, repetition, or customer preference.
- Bind each mandatory statement, institutional fact, identity, title, date, rule,
  responsibility, achievement, commitment, signature/sign-off statement, and
  attachment reference to eligible evidence or explicit customer confirmation at
  the strength the shared contract permits.
- Never use `创作性补全`, `推演内容`, `示意内容`, or `待核实内容` to fabricate an
  official fact, authority, title, identity, date, regulation/institutional rule,
  signature, approval, commitment, performance result, or responsibility.
- Distinguish source fact, interpretation/explanation, and proposed/recommended
  language. A recommendation must not read as an already approved decision or
  institutional commitment.
- Preserve contradictions, missing attachments, unavailable governing text, and
  version uncertainty when they affect formal meaning. Do not resolve them through
  confident wording.
- Keep citations and attachment references locatable and consistent. A cited title,
  section, appendix, table, figure, date, or version must point to the intended bound
  source or be disclosed as unresolved.
- Apply the shared high-risk verification, source protection, output-first, and
  `《待核实内容清单》` rules. Ordinary creative supplements remain possible only
  outside the mandatory and authoritative boundaries above.

## 横向参数默认值

- `input_entry_route`: inherit the established material route
- `use_mode`: `reading` recommendation only after explicit delegation; otherwise inherit or resolve the existing startup choice
- `visual_binding`: inherit the validated visual route
- `evidence_rigor`: `formal`
- `information_density`: `medium`
- `motion_density`: `minimal` recommendation only; require customer selection or explicit delegation
- `continuation_state`: inherit the Handoff decision

These defaults never override a known, confirmed, inherited, or explicitly delegated
horizontal value. `content_length` remains the shared independent choice.

## IR 映射边界

Direct HTML remains the default. Only when the IR engineering route is independently
authorized may this Profile guide existing formal evidence rigor, `claim`, `evidence`,
`narrative_unit`, appendix, citation, and page-task semantics.

Do not add formal-recipient, signature, approval, archive, mandatory-section, or
institutional-rule fields to the Report IR Schema. Do not create a fixed JSON formal-
document model, Profile-triggered IR path, Compiler branch, signature engine, or
records-management implementation. The customer-readable formal state belongs in the
confirmed Report Design Brief; any authorized IR remains downstream.

## Runtime/主题使用

Use only the established reading or presentation mode, validated built-in/project/
enterprise visual route, and the current Runtime. Formal reading may use restrained
composition, clear hierarchy, locatable attachments, and static completeness through
existing page semantics.

The Profile does not add a formal-document theme, signing UI, review workflow,
document-management feature, dual-screen behavior, complex state, or cross-page
morphing. Theme choice remains independent and cannot change the authority of text.

## QA 验收

Run all existing objective QA, traceability, asset/browser QA, current-file
authorization rechecks, Handoff validation, and delivery gates. In addition, verify:

- `required-section completeness`: every actual mandatory chapter, field,
  declaration, required statement, and attachment reference has an accurate visible
  location or an explicitly blocking gap;
- `authority/version`: every governing source has the correct issuer/identity,
  applicability, version/date, effective basis, and checked scope, and no ordinary
  reference has been promoted to authority;
- terminology, figures, dates, identities, versions, scope, responsibilities,
  conclusions, and repeated summary statements remain consistent across sections;
- responsibility, authorship, approval, sign-off, signature, closing-name,
  commitment, and achieved-result wording stays inside confirmed authority;
- every citation, attachment, appendix, section, table, and figure reference resolves
  to the intended location and uses the correct label/version;
- length, format, file, naming, deadline, and source-backed confidentiality or
  distribution constraints are satisfied or visibly disclosed as unresolved;
- facts, interpretation, proposed language, and recommendations remain
  distinguishable in both detailed text and summaries; and
- delivery wording is accurate: the artifact is called ready or formally deliverable
  only when the exact applicable checks pass, while unresolved mandatory facts or
  authority gaps are never hidden by polished language.

Any failure remains a Profile-specific QA failure in addition to the shared gate; it
does not replace or collapse existing QA and delivery conclusions.

## 能力叠加与冲突处理

Research support, periodic data, decision explanation, teaching, or live delivery may
be bounded overlays with a stated source Profile, reason, and affected scope. They may
not import another Profile's complete intake, design-ready gate, narrative sequence,
artifact set, or QA workflow.

Keep this Profile primary when no external eligibility, scoring, or authoritative
item-by-item response rule decides success and the formal recipient/use/record boundary
remains dominant. When authoritative qualification, mandatory response, scoring, or
defense rules determine success, switch primary to
`rule-response-application-defense`, rebuild only affected design decisions, and use
the existing complete-brief confirmation. Never run both Golden Paths in full.
